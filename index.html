Google collaborates with university students who are passionate about growing developer communities and support them with starting student clubs on their campuses.

Why DSC?

Developer Students Club-JIIT is a Google recognized student body at JIIT which directly reports to Google Developers for their activities and is supported by the tech giant.

DSC-JIIT conducts workshops, seminars and other fun activities which help the students connect with Google.
